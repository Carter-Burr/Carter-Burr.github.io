# Mapping Cybersecurity Crime and Events Across the United States

![CyberSecurity](https://www.securitydegreehub.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/usa-cybersecurity-map.jpg)


The GIS project is aimed at **visualizing** and **analyzing** cybersecurity events and attacks across the United States. This project will aim to leverage the power of web based geographic information systems to visualize data on cyber events and attacks. Using advanced mapping technology will help us provide a comprehensive view of the cyberthreat landscape in the United States. Mapping cybersecurity events is very important as it provides a visual representation of the geographic spread of threats, helping educate individuals and organizations about the current cyberthreats to our country. Additionally, this project will also lets us *spot trends and patterns*, which helps us make smarter decisions to keep our digital world safe.


## Why is it important?

The internet has been advancing at a rapid rate that we have never seen before, and with this explosion of advancemnt has come a variety of risks with security. Cyber attacks happen every single day all across the country, and it is important that we are aware of both the severity and the volume of these attacks. Being able to map various cybersecurity events and trends can help educate us on both the past present and future of what the security landscape looks like.

Learn more about why CyberSecurity awarness is important [here](https://www.cybsafe.com/blog/7-reasons-why-security-awareness-training-is-important/#:~:text=It%20helps%20employees%20understand%20the,report%2C%20and%20prevent%20security%20incidents.).
